Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in Paris in 1987 by Jean-Pierre Stackly, our company began with a singular vision: to redefine luxury real estate by connecting discerning individuals with extraordinary properties that transcend mere accommodation.

What started as a boutique agency specializing in French Riviera estates has evolved into a global network spanning 28 countries, yet we've never lost our founding commitment to personalized service and uncompromising quality.

Today, Stackly represents the pinnacle of luxury real estate, with a curated portfolio of over 350 exceptional properties, each hand-selected for its architectural significance, prime location, and investment potential.

Jean-Pierre Stackly Founder & Chairman
Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

1987

The Beginning

Stackly is founded in Paris with a focus on exceptional French Riviera properties. Our first sale: a historic villa in Cap-Ferrat.

1995

European Expansion

Opening offices in London, Monaco, and Geneva, establishing Stackly as Europe's premier luxury property specialist.

2005

Going Global

Expansion into North America, Middle East, and Asia Pacific. Portfolio grows to include iconic properties in Dubai, New York, and Hong Kong.

2015

Digital Innovation

Launch of immersive virtual property tours and blockchain-secured transactions, pioneering digital innovation in luxury real estate.

2024

New Horizons

Celebrating 37 years of excellence with presence in 28 countries, 350+ luxury properties, and a team of 200+ dedicated specialists.
